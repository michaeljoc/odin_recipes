How to make Lemon-Pepper Roasted Broccoli and Cauliflower

Simple roasted broccoli and cauliflower seasoned with lemon-pepper that pairs nicely with steak, chicken, fish, or pork.

Required Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons lemon-pepper seasoning
  • ¼ teaspoon seasoned salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. Combine broccoli, cauliflower, olive oil, lemon-pepper, seasoned salt, and red pepper flakes in a large bowl; stir well. Spread out evenly on a baking sheet.

  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  3. Roast in the preheated oven for 12 minutes. Stir and roast until tender, about 12 minutes more.
